# Media Kit Generator

**Ledger & Spark** — Free tools. No catch. Built because you asked.

## What It Does
Enter your stats, get a professional media kit with auto-calculated sponsorship rates. Screenshot and send to brands.

## How to Use
1. Pick your platform (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Multi-Platform)
2. Enter your stats (followers, views, engagement rate)
3. Pick a style (Minimal, Professional, Bold)
4. Build → screenshot → send to brands
